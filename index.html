Desktop Design

Hardware Overview

Prism Flex’s compact, multi-I/O, low-power design can squeeze into small spaces – making it easy to integrate into any live production workspace and workflow. It includes a 12G-SDI input and loop through, HDMI 2.0 input, dual Ethernet ports, WiFi 6, and 2 USB ports for LTE modems and external recording drives.*

*Decoder includes 12G-SDI and HDMI 2.0 Outputs
